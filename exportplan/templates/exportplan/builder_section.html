Target markets

Identify the markets that will most likely respond to your product or service. You will learn how to research and assess potential markets from a range of different methods.

 

Which countries are you targeting, with which products or services, and why? Use research to identify evidence of both market attractiveness, and ease of operating in the market.

Some examples of market attractiveness are:

  • evidence of consumer demand
  • identifying a unique selling point (USP) for your product in the market

Some examples of market ease are:

  • ease of doing business in the market (World Bank score)
  • the level of adaptation required for your product and packaging, or service, to sell it in the market
